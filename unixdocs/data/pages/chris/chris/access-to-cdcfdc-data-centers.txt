===== Acccess to CDC and FDC Data Centers =====

   To open a ticket to get access to the CDC - Chicago Ddata Center  or the FDC - Failover Datacenter in Philidelphhia follow the proceedure below
   Send an email to SFLD-SDCOperators@federalmogul.com. Include the individuals that you are requesting access for so that they have the information available when they go onsite
   In the email subject Line enter the following information:
   Data Center CDC or FDC 
   Date access is requested for 
   People you are requesting acces for 
   The reason for access
    In the body of the text, list further details including contact details for the individuals who need access.
 The contact details will be checked when they arrive on site. 
The contact information should include the following:
persons name - as it appears on their drivers license
persons contact information - cell Phome Number 
persons e-mail address
