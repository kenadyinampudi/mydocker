====== F1500 SVP Login procedures ======


  * Use RDP to log into the SVP @ 10.131.10.65
  * Click on the "SVP" button.
  * Use "maintenance" and password from secret server for the initial prompt.
  * On the second prompt, use the password in the notes for the SVP entry in secret server.
  * To reboot, bring up "run" and type "shutdown -r"
  * Don't forget the "-r" option for reboot, otherwise you just power off the SVP.

You should now have access to the SVP.
