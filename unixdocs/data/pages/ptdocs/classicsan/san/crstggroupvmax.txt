==== Notes ====

  * Login to VMAX Unisphere.
{{san:01volumes.png|}}
  * Click on Storage - Storage Groups - then click on "Create"
{{:san:01crstggrp.png|}}
  * Give it a name and choose "Manual Selection". Click Next.
{{:san:02crstggrp.png|}}
  * At this screen - click on filter ( the wine glass )
{{:san:03crstggrp.png|}}
  * Enter the following filters and click ok.
    * Capacity Range - 126G - 140G
    * Emulation - FBA
    * Meta - Striped
{{:san:04crstggrp.png|}}
  * Hold CTRL key, click on both devices and then click on Add to Group, then click Next.
{{:san:05crstggrp.png|}}
  * At the summary screen, click Finish.
{{:san:06crstggrp.png|}}
