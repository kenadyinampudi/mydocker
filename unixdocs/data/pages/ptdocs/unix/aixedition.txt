====== Display the edition of AIX - Enterprise, Standard, or Express ======

  - To list the current edition on the system<code>sudo chedition –l
chedition -list</code>One of the following outputs will be returned:<code>express | standard | enterprise</code>
  - To change to the standard edition, type:<code>sudo chedition –s<code>To change to express<code>sudo chedition -exp</code>
