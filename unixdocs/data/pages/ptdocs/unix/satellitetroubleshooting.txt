====== Satellite Troubleshooting ======
===== Subscriptions seem to take up more subscriptions than necessary =====
This is because when you buy one "subscription", you are given two "entitlements".  You can use one entitlement per VM, or you can use two entitlements per one physical server. \\ 
So, consuming two "entitlements" is behavior by design, because of terminology.
